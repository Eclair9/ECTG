Welcome to Eclair Cherry Tree Guide!




Dive into the enchanting world of CherryTree, a captivating text-based RPG that balances simplicity with depth, allowing players to embark on thrilling adventures filled with unique challenges and rewards. This guide serves as your comprehensive resource for mastering every aspect of the game.

Explore a variety of skills including Attack, Strength, Fishing, and Crafting, each designed to enhance your gameplay as you aim for the prestigious levels of 99 or even 130. Encounter formidable enemies that drop rare loot, and take on engaging quests and slayer tasks that offer bountiful rewards. As you progress, unlock powerful gear, elixirs, and hidden treasures that elevate your gaming experience.

Our guide is meticulously organized, featuring dedicated sections for upgrades, skills, items, and treasure hunts. Whether you're looking to enhance your character’s abilities, strategize your next quest, or discover the secrets of powerful items, Eclair CherryTree Guide has you covered.

Join our community of passionate players and share your adventures as you navigate the intricate mechanics and strategies of CherryTree. With our help, you'll quickly learn the ropes and ultimately master the game.

Advancement Guide


Grinding for Silver Coins and Ring Fragments

Kill some Cobras or Fluffies in the Slayer Dungeon while mining iron ore. Use the iron ore to craft a large amount of steel bars, then use the steel bars to craft several Spiked Shields. Finally, sell the Spiked Shields for silver coins.



Grinding for Gold Coins

This is more of an end-game or late-game build. Relying only on Gold Touch Potions offers a limited chance to earn gold. Most players nowadays use the Opulina Set with all Wishing Wells maxed out. For now, try finding rare items from caskets to sell, or consider working for someone in tasks like farming and exploration. You can also join the CherryTree Discord channel to find potential employers.
